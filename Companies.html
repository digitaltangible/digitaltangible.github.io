Companies


Editing catalogs, commercial information or brochures with us is very easy and cheap: we can produce your company´s Seebook cards for €1 the unit (with a minimum run of 500 copies) or, with simpler characteristics, for €0.65 the unit (with a minimum run of 1000). In Digital Tangible we will be in charge of printing the cards to suit your taste, the secure hosting of your digital files and the maintenance of the application the allows the download.


Seebook is an ideal format for editing commercial information of your company. It is cheaper than printing extensive paper catalogs and it gives you valuable information about the clients that are interested in your product, since you will know from which emails downloads were made.


It is likely that your company provides business information to be distributed to several contacts, but that you do not want to expose publicly throughout a website of public access. It could be confidential information (sales margins, salaries...) or excessively detailed information to engage the public in general (catalogs of parts, technical requirements...) Currently many businesses use tablets where they can view information: big printed catalogs are becoming less necessary. Many are never consulted o go to the waste basket: a considerable expense and useless.


Seebook lets you control who receives your information, we record the email of the recipient when it performs the download, for your company. If you want a more tighter control of the distribution of the company´s content, you can provide us a list with only emails from which you want to allow the downloads. We will make sure that now anyone that is not in the list can access the files on your behalf.


With Seebook you can lower the printing costs of catalogs and other extensive information.


With Seebook you will have something to hand to dealers, colleagues or potential clients: it is more favorable to your company´s image than just handing a download link.


With Seebook you can keep your commercial information up to date: one card lets you always access the last version of a particular file.


Seebooks are very light, so you will be reducing the freight costs of extensive catalogs.


If you want to know in detail the advantages that Seebook has to offer o if you would like to place an order, contact us. You will receive the cards of your work printed in professional quality in a few days. Seebook puts at the service of your work a totally secure and reliable download application.
