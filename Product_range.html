Product Range


Seebook Standard:

12 x 18 cm brochure (closed size).

High quality printing in 300 gr cardboard, with low gloss coating.

Includes ExLib system (embedded watermark in the ebook).

Includes serial number.

Standard size with a blank space, suitable to be sent as a post card.

Delivered sealed (seal made of tearable paper).

Especially recommended for publishers and selfpublished authors.
