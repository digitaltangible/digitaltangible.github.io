Authors


Publishing with us is easy and economical. In Digital Tangible we charge you a fixed price per card (€1 in standard mode or €0.65 in the simplest form: see our Product Range). As an author, you decide the final retail price you want them to have. Naturally you can also offer your readers a free Seebook so people can get to know a sample of your work.


We will print them to suit your taste and we will send them to you. We will also manage the secure hosting of your digital files and the maintenance of the software application that allows downloading.


Seebook is an ideal format for selfpublishing. It´s cheaper than printing a book. And if you already have an ebook in an open sales platform (Amazon or similar), Seebook will provide the additional tangible touch that any book needs. Always have several Seebooks with you available for potential readers!


Moreover, with Seebook the possibilities of promoting your work multiplies:


Seebook has a blank space that lets you sign or dedicate your own books, even if they are digital. This comes in handy for presentations or lectures!



You decide the prize and the conditions of the sale: you can sell them after a presentation or lecture or give them as a gift to your most loyal readers.


They are very light, so you can take hundreds of them with you to your presentations or trips. (100 ebooks have around the same weight as a hardcover book).




You can send them by snail mail! They have the format of a postcard and a blank space to write the recipient address.




If you want to know in detail the benefits that Seebook offers you or if you want to place an order, contact us. We can send you a promotional Seebook so you can try our system by downloading an explanatory ebook.
