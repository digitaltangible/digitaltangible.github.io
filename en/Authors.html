Authors


Are you an author? Have you published an ebook?


Congratulations! To write a book is a huge challenge that not many manage to master.


But now you probably want to be read. And you may find out soon that it is not enough to make your ebook available on a sales platform. You may want to have something in hand that you can give as a present, to sell or to sign to your readers on the talks or presentations you will organize in order to promote it.


Seebook Publicity can help you!

We will print them to suit your taste and manage the secure hosting of your digital files and the maintenance of the software application that allows downloading.


Seebook Publicity is an ideal format for selfpublishing. ItÂ´s cheaper than printing a book. And if you already have an ebook in a sales platform (Amazon or similar), Seebook will provide the tangibility that a book also should have. Always have several Seebooks with you available for potential readers!


Moreover, with Seebook the possibilities of promoting your work multiplies:


In order to download your e-book, your readers will have to introduce their email addresses. Our service allows you to send them an email later on to announce them the publication of your new book, to tell them about your next talks or to offer them a subscription to your blog or your newsletter.


A direct contact with your readers that a conventional sales platform won't offer you!


Seebook has a blank space that lets you sign or dedicate your own books, even if they are digital. This comes in handy for presentations or lectures!



You decide the prize and the conditions of the sale: you can sell them after a presentation or lecture or give them as a gift to your most loyal readers.


They are very light, so you can take hundreds of them with you to your presentations or trips. (100 ebooks have around the same weight as a hardcover book).




You can send them by snail mail! They have the format of a postcard and a blank space to write the recipient address.
