Frequently Asked Questions (faq)


Is Seebook a sales platform?

Seebook is not a sales platform like Amazon, for its distribution it uses the traditional sales mechanisms used for paperbooks (brick and morter shops).

Can I download my Seebook file in any part of the world?

Yes. When you buy a Seebook card in its point of sales, you would be able to immediately download your ebook in any part of the globe and at any time, without restrictions.

How many times can I download it?

Seebook does not limit the amount of times you can download your file. You can download it in as many devices as you want and in different formats. In case you loose the digital file you can get a new download link as long as you keep using the same email.

Why must I keep the Seebook card once I downloaded the ebook?

Because it does not cost a thing: it does not occupy any space and it is pretty. And because while you keep your card you will be able to re-claim the download of your ebook as long as you keep using the same email you used at the original download. This way the loss or damage of your reading device does not imply the loss of the file it contained.

In addition, if the publisher or the author decides to make corrections or improvements to his ebook, with your Seebook card you will always be able to access the latest version.

Finally, each card has a serial number. If you purchase the first edition in Seebook and the book turns out to be famous, with time your Seebook may have value as a collectible item, especially if you purchased the first edition of the series.

Is my content secure in Seebook?

Seebook guarantees the confidentiality and compliance with the Law of Intellectual Property of all the content hosted on their server. Our application for downloads is totally reliable and performs through a secure https protocol. Likewise we guarantee under a contract the confidentiality of the content to our suppliers, whether they are publishers, companies or independent artist, ensuring at all time the custody of the hosted content. The company DIGITAL TANGIBLE, SL, of which Seebook is a trademark, has a declared file in the Data Protection Agency of Spain that guarantees its exclusive use for the purpose stated.

In what formats can I do the downloads?

Once you scanned the QR inside the card you will access a page requesting the email we will forward the download link to. You will be able to choose the desired format among the ones available: PDF, EPUB, or MOBI. Best of all: if you change your device or your opinion, you just need to scan the QR again to choose a different format.
