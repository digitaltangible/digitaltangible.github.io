What is an Exlib?


The ExLib is our technological update of a classic element: the ex libris. Formerly, as books were valuable and expensive commodities, every good reader had his particular ex libris designed and printed with his name. He used to paste it in the first page of a book as a sign that it was his. That way he could lend it without the new reader forgetting who to give it back to.



Our ExLib is the picture you will see inside our Seebooks. It carries the serial number of the card and the codes you will need to download the content.

We wanted to incorporate the original ex libris philosophy in our Seebooks, but adapting them to today´s world: our ExLib will also personalize your ebook, but with your “digital identity”, i.e. with your email address.



The ExLibs are distributed randomly among our Seebooks. Till you open it, you will not know which one is inside your card. And as we want you to remember us, there will always be a fish in some part of the picture: in honour to "our" fish .

We order our Exlibs from designers and artists. We will announce the new drawings and promote the works of our chosen artists in our social networks. From time to time we will carry out contests in fine arts and designing schools to award and post the winning picture in some of our Seebooks.


Do you want to create an ExLib?


Do you teach at a fine art school and would like to organize a contest among your students? Are you an artist or a designer with certain track record and you would like to form part of our project designing an ExLib for us? If that´s the case, contact us!

If we like it, we will add it to our gallery of ExLibs and promote it throughout our social networks.

Remember, there is only one condition: the drawing must have a fish!
