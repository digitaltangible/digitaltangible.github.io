Musicians


Seebook is perfect for publishing music. It is cheaper than burning a CD, but has still all the advantages of the tangible.



Currently, one of the main sources of income for artists are live performances. It's important that sales can be made on the spot, at the music event itself, seizing the audiences motivation to buy. It is therefore necessary to have a physical format, and Seebook offers many advantages over the CD:


Being cheaper to print than a CD, the final sales price can be much lower.


More and more people only want to hear music through mobile devices. Purchasing a CD requires the cumbersome task of ripping the musical files. With Seebook, your themes can be downloaded in MP3 on the spot.


With Seebook you can download the songs, but also a PDF with the lyrics and even a videoclip! Test it yourself by following the instructions of this picture or clicking directly here.



With Seebook you can also provide an after-show-record of a concert! Just sell the previously printed Seebook cards at the concert place itself. We will send an email with the download link to every buyer as soon as the record is ready for them, minutes or hours later. Much cheaper and easier than with towers of CD burners... Ask for our conditions!


Musicians can sign Seebook cards after a concert.


Each time you ask, but at least once a year, we will send you an updated download report, containing a lot of valuable data to let you know your public better: when, where and in which format your music has been downloaded, which device has been used...


Seebook offers many possibilities as a promotion tool. For example, you can produce a Seebook with only a few sample songs redirecting to the link where you can purchase the whole album.


With Seebook you can develop strategies of segmented publicity. You can keep all the downloaders of your Seebooks posted about new concerts or music releases.


We can also provide you a file with the codes (QR + alphanumeric), so you can print them on your own material or on concert tickets.

If you want to know more, just contact us!
