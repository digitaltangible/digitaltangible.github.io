Musicians


Editing music with us has never been easier. In Digital Tangible we will take care of printing the cards according to your taste, the shipping, the secure hosting of your digital files and the maintenance of the application that allows its download.


Seebook is an ideal format for musicians self publishing. It is cheaper than burning a CD and it offers more visibility than online publishing.





Currently, one of the main sources of income for artists are live performances. It's important that sales can be made on the spot, at the music event itself, seizing the audiences motivation to buy. It is therefore necessary to have a physical format, and Seebook offers many advantages over the CD:


Being cheaper to print than a CD, the final sales price can be much lower.


More and more people only want to hear music through MP3 recorders or similar. Purchasing a CD requires the cumbersome task of ripping the musical files. With Seebook, you can download it immediately.


Our system accepts multiple formats. With Seebook you can download different songs separately, but also a pdf with the lyrics and even a short videoclip! Test it yourself by clicking here or scan the QR code of our sample card:




With Seebook you can also provide an after-show-sale of the concert recording! Just sell the previously printed Seebook cards at the concert place itself. We will send an email with the download link to every buyer as soon as the record is ready for them, minutes or hours later. Even internationally! Get in touch with us and we will provide you the QR code that you have to print on your cards. Send us the file and we will do the rest! It will be much cheaper and easier than with towers of CD burners. Ask for our conditions!


Seebook offers a free space for the musicians to sign after the concert.


Seebook offers many possibilities as a promotion tool. For example, you can edit a Seebook that only contains a few songs as a sample you can give away after a concert, redirecting to the link where you can purchase the whole album.


With Seebook you can develop strategies of segmented publicity. You can keep all the downloaders of your Seebooks posted about your new concerts or music releases.


Seebooks are very light, so you can take hundreds of them to your gigs or tours.

If you want to learn in detail all the benefits that Seebook has to offer or if you want to place an order, contact us. In a couple of days you will receive your Seebook cards at home, printed in professional quality. We also can provide you a png with the QR code along with the alphanumeric number, so you can print it yourself wherever you want: on the tickets for the concert, on your own cards or on flyers. We will take care of the file hosting and user notification.
