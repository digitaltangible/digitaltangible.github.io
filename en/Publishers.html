Publishers


Publishing with us is easy. As a publisher you decide the final retail price that will be printed on your card. In Digital Tangible we will print them to suite your taste, with your logo clearly visible on the front side. But above all, we will be in charge of the secure hosting of your digital files, of incorporating the fingerprinting (Exlib) and of maintaining the IT infrastructure that makes the download available.

Seebook addresses many of the challenges that the digital era proposes to the publishing world. A Seebook allows you to:


Maintain the book value chain and gives new opportunities to brick-and-mortar stores.


Have a verification of print-runs thanks to the serial numbers.




Give visibility to your published ebooks in the bookstores, at the price that you decide.


The publication, under your brand, of those authors you consider of good quality, but too risky to be traditionally published in paper. As a publisher you can ensure them the presence in physical bookshops that a conventional ebook can not offer.




Having reliable statistics of the downloads (what title, where and when).


Having an ebook as a physical attractive object that can easily become an impulse purchase.


Getting your ebooks bought as a gift. (According to statistics, gifts make around 50% of the traditional paper book sales).




New and original promotional strategies: surprise Seebooks, Seebooks previously signed by the author, stamped Seebooks to be send by mail, etc.


Discouraging piracy thanks to our Exlib fingerprinting concept, without the hostility that DRM generates in several readers.


If you are interested in adding this concept to your catalogue, contact us. We can send you a promotional Seebook to let you try our system.
