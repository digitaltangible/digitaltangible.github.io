An ebook you can touch

Seebook is a download card for ebooks.



With the codes inside the card, you can download your ebook in any usual format (ePub, Mobi for Kindle or PDF), from whatever device (tablet, smartphone or PC).


You can use your Seebook card as many times as you want, and even download the same book in more than one format.

Do you have a friend you used to give books for birthday and you cannot anymore because now she only reads digitally? You could get her a gift card but... is just not so personal, and you want to give her that book and not any other.

With Seebook, you can gift wrap your chosen book and write her a note on the free space inside.

The ebooks you download with Seebook are not DRM protected: they just wear a whatermark. With Seebook, you own the file you get!


You can easily send it by mail, since Seebooks have no geographic restrictions. So your friends can read them anywhere in the world.

As in a DVD, in many of our Seebooks you might find things like a podcast from the author exclusively recorded for you, images related to the book, rejected chapters...



Because in Seebook we valuate writing nas we know that creating a book implies a little universe that is worth comunicating.


Would you like to get one? You can buy them on our more than 150 bookstores all over Spain or in our online shop.

¿Por qué Seebook?

Actualmente tenemos tres opciones para leer un libro:

Opción a: el libro en papel


Opción b: el ebook


Opción c: nuestro Seebook, que es digital y tangible a la vez.



Por eso nos habría gustado llamarlo c-book, pero en castellano eso suena a bóvido asiático.

Pronunciado en inglés, en cambio, tenemos Seebook.

Pues bien: see nos lleva a 'ver', pero también a sea, que es el mar.

De ahí que nuestro logo sea… ¡un pez de ojos grandes!

It is simple. Inside Seebook there is a QR code you can scan with QRreader or any other application on your Android or iPhone. (If you cannot scan it, do not worry!: you will also find an alphanumeric code that your can introduce directly in our web digitaltangible.com).



You will only have to choose the format you would like for your ebook (EPUB, MOBI or PDF), introduce your email and that is it! In your inbox you will find the link that will lead you to our download page. There you will get your ebook in only one click.


But that's not all! You can also download the extra materials that enrich many of our Seebooks.



Try to keep your Seebook card: that way you can ask for your ebook again in case you lost it. Keeping it will be no nuisance: it hardly occupies any space, it does not weight anything, it is as pretty as a book cover and it is numbered, so you will be the only owner. And think that one day it can become a collectors item! Specially if it is dedicated by the author or it corresponds to the first numbers of the series.









Why "Seebook"?


Because today, when it comes to reading a book, you have three options:

Option a: the usual paper book



Option b: the actual ebook



Option c: our Seebook, that is tangible and digital at the same time.



We would love to call it c-book, but in Spanish there is no way to pronounce that nicely. So we chose Seebook, that sounds exactly the same ans looks nicer, right?

Now: see takes us to "look", but there is also sea...



and that is why our logo is… a fish with big eyes!


We liked the fish especially because our company was born in the Mediterranean harbour of Barcelona, because we like the sea and because each time we walk by our favourite corner of Barcelona Olímpica we come across this fish from Frank Gehry we just love... and that remarkably looks like ours:




The Mission


Seebook is our proposal to establish a bridge between the traditional physical editions and the digital format that:

Provides a tool to the traditional publishing industry to adapt to the digital era.

Invigorates the cultural market generating a third dissemination channel.

Facilitates authors and musicians the promotion of their work.

Brings the digital world to a generation more used to the tangible.
