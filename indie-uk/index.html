Have you published an e-book?

Congratulations!

We know how hard it is.

Now the time to get readers has come!!!

Promote: create a blog, feed you social networking sites, gain followers...

And above all, get the word out. Talk about your book every chance you get.

But...

If you bump into your neighbour on the lift and s/he is interested in buying your book, are you going to suggest that s/he goes to Amazon?

Ten minutes later they won’t even remember the title.

Or perhaps they don’t have an account in Amazon.

Or they are not digital readers and don’t know what mobi or ePub files are.

Are you going to give up on all those readers?

And… how will you sign it?

Traditional writers organise book launches and invite their fans, friends and relations.

In just that one day they sell dozens of books and sign many copies.

You’d love to be able to sign your book too.

But yours is an e-book and you don’t have anything you can sign or dedicate… Or do you?

Tangibility matters!

In the real world you need to promote your book in a tangible way.

You need to be able to hand something out.

Something you can inscribe.

Or sell right there and then.

Or wrap up as a present.

People attach more value to the things they can touch and feel.

We make your e-book tangible
