The audioguide you can take with you

Have this ever happened to you?

You want to go and see an exhibition, but you have only time for a quick visit.

You wonder if it worthy to spend money on the audioguide if you can only listen about 20% of all the information contained therein.

Besides, you don't like to use devices which have been touched by too many hands.

Eventually you decide to give up, missing important information which would have enriched your visit.

This also happens to your museum visitors!

Many people would pay for the audioguide if besides of use it in the museum they were able to take it home with them to continue listening later.

Or if they could access to it directly from their mobile.

Or save it as a permanent souvenir from their visit.

Or purchase the audioguides of previous exhibitions which they were unable to visit.

Seebook has the solution:

An attractive card for download, which inner codes allows direct access to the sound tracks.

Without having to program expensive apps.

Incorporating the languages you need.

Protecting the content using restricted codes to one or more devices.

Able to be heard on streaming (with low consumption of data, memory and battery).

Easily adaptable to any change in the content.

With the ability to incorporate other documents: videos, location maps, PDFs...

Tangibility matters!

Printable surface of our audioguide can make permanent a temporary exhibition.

Favors the branding of your museum.

It allows you to print the logos of your sponsors and incentivize their support.

You could give for free to prominent visitors or mail it for promotional purposes.

And it may subsequently be sold in the museum store.

Make permanent your audioguide and transform it into an audiopublication!

Because people attributes more value to what they can touch.

Do you want to know how old your visitor is?

Our audioguide collects statistical data (type of device, heard tracks, language, duration of the visit, date and time, etc.).

But also, we ask directly to the visitor the age, gender, if it is the first time coming to the museum and their opinion on what they thought of the exhibition.

And that just at the right moment: at the end of their visit, inside the museum and without sending the visitor to none external link. We make it so easy that the visitor will respond!

Our audioguide becomes a source of data about your visitors. Rebuild the visitor's journey of your audience without invasive methods and completely anonymously!

We make your audioguide tangible

Each one of our cards have an unique code and is not transferable.

In Seebook we will take care of everything needed for you just only have to think about your exhibition.

We will translate the texts and locutions.

We will layout and print the cards.

You will be able to choose your card between two formats: large diptych card or smart card of PVC.

If you wish, we provide headphones and we will customize them with your logo.

We will send you statistical data regarding to the use of the audioguide and user feedback.
